About

I am an Engineering Data Science graduate from the University of Houston, specialized in Deep Learning, Text Mining, and Information Visualization. My academic pursuits at both University of Houston and Hochschule Stralsund have provided me with a deep understanding of Applied Mathematics and Vehicle Management Systems, positioning me at the forefront of AI and ML innovation.

Currently, as a Research Assistant at the University of Houston, I excel in fine-tuning Large Language Models and enhancing multi-GPU system performance. My key projects involve sophisticated data processing and embedding techniques for Wikipedia English data, significantly advancing the field of text analytics in AI research.

My practical deep learning experience includes implementing advanced semantic segmentation models at Volkswagen AG and developing an OCR system at Armor Solar Power Films GmbH. These roles have honed my skills in applying deep learning to diverse real-world applications, from vehicle aerodynamics to solar module analysis.
